Story behind TheoreX

The story starts with Ayan Choudhury, a simple boy from Kolkata, who just like the majority of students in India, choose to pursue Engineering after his schools. Two years down the line overburdened and frustrated with the monotonous theories of IT with almost no scope of practical implementations slowly started thinking differently. He embarks on a new dream as he starts meeting young souls from different open source communities. After completing his B.Tech he joins a startup, where inspired with the scope to learn new things, he decides to have something of his own. En route he meets Sumantro Mukherjee and Debapriya Bhattacharya, two lovely young guys always bustling with new thoughts and ideas. They join hands, brainstorm ideas, believe and live their dreams and thus came up TheoreX-Theory & Experience.
